Selected Layer

Nothing selected — click a layer on the key or in the layer list.

Clip Mask

Clearances are added around the keyring hole and touch pad. All values in mm. Pad clearance applies to the front only.

▸ calibrate key geometry (mm)

Y is measured from the tip of the USB-C end. Measure your key with calipers for a perfect fit, then re-check the dashed clip lines.

Export

PNG is written at true physical scale (DPI embedded) — canvas is exactly the key's real size. Black = engrave, white = skip. Import into LightBurn / LaserGRBL and it lands at real size.

Sticker (Cricut)

Exports the active side (FRONT) as a die-cut sticker: your clip-mask shape becomes the cut line, including the keyring hole and touch pad cut-outs. SVG has the printed design plus a red cut path — in Cricut Design Space, flatten the image layer and set the red path to Cut. PNG is the same artwork at true scale for print-then-cut.
